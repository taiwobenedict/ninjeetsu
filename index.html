How To Buy
Create a Wallet

Download Phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or Google Play for free. For desktop users, download the Google Chrome extension by visiting Phantom's website.

Get some SOL

Ensure you have SOL in your wallet to switch to $ninjeetsu. If you don't have any SOL, you can buy it directly on Phantom, transfer it from another wallet, or purchase it on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Raydium

Connect to Raydium. Open Raydium in Google Chrome or on your browser within your Phantom app. Connect your wallet, paste the ninjeetsu token address into Raydium, select $ninjeetsu, and confirm when Phantom prompts you for a wallet.

Ninjeetsu

Exchange SOL for $ninjeetsu. We have zero taxes, so you don't need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may choose to.
